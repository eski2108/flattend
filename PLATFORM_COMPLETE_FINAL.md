## üîÑ **Transaction Flow Examples**

### P2P Trade Flow with Notifications:

```
1. Seller creates offer (0.1 BTC @ ¬£50k)
   ‚Üí Seller gets notification: "Offer created successfully"

   ‚Üí Buyer: "Trade created, escrow being locked"

   ‚Üí Seller: "Your crypto is in escrow"

   ‚Üí Seller: "New message from Buyer"

   ‚Üí Taker fee deducted: ¬£25 (1% of ¬£2,500)
   ‚Üí Admin gets ¬£20 (80%)
   ‚Üí Referrer gets ¬£5 (20%) [if seller has referrer]
   ‚Üí Buyer: "Payment marked as sent"
   ‚Üí Seller: "‚ö†Ô∏è Buyer claims payment sent - check your account"

5. Seller clicks "Release Crypto"
   ‚Üí Maker fee deducted: 0.0005 BTC (1%)
   ‚Üí Admin gets 0.0004 BTC (80%)
   ‚Üí Referrer gets 0.0001 BTC (20%) [if seller has referrer]
   ‚Üí Buyer receives 0.0495 BTC (0.05 - 0.0005 fee)
   ‚Üí Buyer: "üéâ Trade completed! You received 0.0495 BTC"
   ‚Üí Seller: "‚úÖ Crypto released - trade complete"
```
